Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

⚖️ Legal Notice: This privacy policy has been reviewed for general compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and app store requirements. However, it should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before production deployment to ensure compliance with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction.

1. Introduction

Enatbet ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Enatbet mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, phone number, profile photo
  • Profile Information: Bio, verification documents (government ID for host verification)
  • Listing Information: Property details, photos, location, pricing
  • Booking Information: Check-in/check-out dates, guest count, special requests
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe. We do not store full credit card numbers, CVV codes, or raw payment card data. Only tokenized payment references are retained.
  • Communications: Messages between guests and hosts, customer support interactions

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, app version
  • Location Information: Approximate location (city-level) to show nearby properties. We request location permission only when needed and you can disable it in device settings.
  • Usage Information: Pages viewed, search queries, booking history, feature interactions
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, referring URLs, crash reports
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns. You can opt-out in Settings.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication: If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • Payment Processing: Transaction status and metadata from Stripe (no raw payment card data)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Process bookings and facilitate payments between guests and hosts
  • Verify host identities and ensure platform safety (age verification, identity checks)
  • Send transactional communications (booking confirmations, updates, customer support messages)
  • Personalize your experience and show relevant property listings based on search history and preferences
  • Prevent fraud, detect abuse, and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Send promotional emails and notifications (you can opt-out anytime)
  • Conduct analytics and research to improve our Service

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your public profile (name, photo, bio), listings, and reviews are visible to other users
  • With Hosts/Guests: When you book, hosts see your name, profile, and contact information. Guests see host contact information after booking confirmation.
  • With Service Providers:
    • Firebase/Google Cloud (authentication, database hosting, storage, analytics)
    • Stripe (payment processing under their Data Processing Agreement)
    • Cloud infrastructure providers for hosting and delivery
  • For Legal Reasons: To comply with laws, court orders, subpoenas, or valid government requests
  • Business Transfers: If Enatbet is acquired, merged, or undergoes asset sale, user data may be transferred to the acquiring entity
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing (e.g., social media integration)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

  • Active Accounts: Retained indefinitely while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days; some retained for legal compliance
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Legal Holds: Data subject to legal proceedings retained until resolution

You can request deletion by contacting us at privacy@enatbet.app. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Update: View and edit your account information in Settings
  • Delete Account: Request account deletion via Settings or email privacy@enatbet.app
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional emails via the link in any email or in Settings
  • Location Services: Disable location access in your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Location Services; Android: Settings > Apps > Permissions)
  • Analytics Opt-Out: Disable analytics tracking in app Settings
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format (JSON)
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information in your profile

6.1 EU/UK Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have additional rights:

  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

6.2 California Users (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used/shared
  • Delete personal information (with certain exceptions)
  • Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

To exercise these rights, email privacy@enatbet.app with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS 1.2+. Data at rest encrypted using AES-256.
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication with secure token-based sessions
  • Payment Security: Stripe's PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant infrastructure (we never handle raw card data)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • Monitoring: Regular security audits, intrusion detection, and vulnerability scanning
  • Incident Response: Data breach notification procedures in place per applicable laws

Important: No system is 100% secure. You are responsible for protecting your account password and enabling two-factor authentication if available.

8. Children's Privacy

Enatbet is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Our Terms of Service prohibit users under 18 from creating accounts. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child's information is on our platform should contact us at privacy@enatbet.app.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure adequate protection for international transfers through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Data Processing Agreements with our service providers (Firebase, Stripe)
  • Adequate security measures per GDPR Article 46

By using our Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential Cookies: Keep you logged in, remember preferences (required for service functionality)
  • Analytics Cookies: Firebase Analytics to understand usage patterns (can be disabled in Settings)
  • Performance Cookies: Monitor app performance and identify technical issues

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly. Mobile app tracking can be controlled via device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking; Android: Settings > Google > Ads).

11. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., social media, property management tools). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or service features. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Material changes will be notified via:

  • Email to your registered address
  • In-app notification
  • Prominent notice on our website

Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
